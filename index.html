What can your team of four do in minecraft in 24 hours?

On the XXX of XXX 2012 at 2pm (GMT), the first craft day will begin, you will have 24 hours, the full minecraft inventory and a field of 200 x 200 blocks to build the best creation you can in the world of minecraft.

Entry to this event is free but limited to 500 teams, this is an online event which means you can enter from anywhere in the WORLD!

On the morning of the event you will be given login details to your minecraft world and the coordiantes of your build area, then when the clock strikes 2pm, you can begin building anything you want in your 24 hours.

Once the 24 hours is up, building is stopped and the public have one week to vote on the top 10 in each catagory (we will be recording the build and allowing people to visit the worlds after the build is over)

Then our juding panel chooses the winner in each catagory who will win some (to be determined prizes).
